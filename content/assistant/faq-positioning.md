People ask whether I'm a backend engineer or an AI engineer. Both, deliberately: Java and Spring Boot production systems are my foundation — that's where I learned what "holds up under real usage" means — and applied AI is where I've pushed over the last year. The combination is the point. RAG systems are mostly backend problems wearing an AI hat: ingestion pipelines, retrieval quality, API design, cost control. My hackathon win and this site's assistant are both that thesis in practice.
