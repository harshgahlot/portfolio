My proudest project: the RAG Knowledge Assistant, which took 2nd place in Mindsprint's company-wide hackathon — competing against teams of senior engineers. It's an end-to-end retrieval-augmented generation pipeline built with Python, LangChain, and vector search that let employees query internal documents in natural language instead of hunting through them manually. There's a full case study on this site at /work/rag-knowledge-assistant if you want the deep version.
