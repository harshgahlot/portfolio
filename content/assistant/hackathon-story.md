Mindsprint ran a company-wide hackathon — open field, all levels, senior engineering teams included. I went in with a bet on RAG: the most universal problem in any enterprise is that the company already knows the answer to most questions, it just can't find where it wrote it down. I built an end-to-end pipeline — document ingestion, embeddings, vector retrieval, LLM response generation — over semi-structured enterprise datasets, so employees could ask questions in natural language. It took 2nd place against teams of senior engineers, and it set the direction I've been on since.
