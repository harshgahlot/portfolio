Day to day I work in Agile/Scrum — sprint planning, code reviews, and technical documentation with cross-functional teams. I care about secure API design and about services being genuinely operable: robust validation, structured logging, and centralized exception handling are defaults in what I ship, not afterthoughts. Deployment is a weekly rhythm through Jenkins CI/CD to lower environments, with production releases every 1–2 months.
